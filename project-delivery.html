Project Goal

This project examined delivery performance to understand the operational factors behind delays. The main goal was to find patterns across weather, traffic, and regional conditions that could help improve delivery efficiency.

Process

  • Data ExplorationReviewed delivery-time records and operational variables to understand the structure of delays.
  • Cleaning and TransformationPrepared the dataset for analysis by organizing categories and standardizing relevant fields.
  • Pattern AnalysisCompared traffic conditions, weather situations, and local delivery context to identify bottlenecks.
  • Insight PresentationConverted patterns into practical findings that could support better operational decisions.

Outcome

  • Highlighted the strongest drivers behind delivery delays.
  • Created a foundation for better planning under high-traffic or weather-sensitive conditions.
  • Improved understanding of how multiple operational factors combine to affect delivery performance.
