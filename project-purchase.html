Project Goal

The goal of this project was to build a model that estimates whether a customer is likely to purchase a product. The work focused on turning customer-related inputs into actionable predictions for marketing and decision support.

Process

  • Data ReviewExplored customer-related variables and checked how they connect to purchase behavior.
  • PreparationHandled encoding, cleaning, and structuring of features for modeling.
  • Model BuildingTested classification approaches to estimate purchase probability.
  • InterpretationReviewed influential variables to understand which factors were driving intent.

Outcome

  • Built a predictive workflow for customer purchase estimation.
  • Generated insights that can support audience targeting and campaign planning.
  • Combined technical modeling with business-oriented interpretation of results.
